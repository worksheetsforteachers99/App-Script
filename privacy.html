Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Your privacy is important. This app uses Google OAuth to access your Google Drive, Docs, and Forms only to create and store worksheets you generate. We do not store or share any personal data.

Data Access

The app requests permission to:

  • Create and view files in your Google Drive (drive.file scope).
  • Manage Google Docs and Forms that it creates.

Data Usage

All worksheet files remain in your Google Drive. We do not read, transmit, or share any document contents outside your account.

Contact

If you have questions, please open an issue on GitHub or email you@example.com.
